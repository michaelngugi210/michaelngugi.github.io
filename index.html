About QuickRetail

QuickRetail is a modern Kenyan retail chain focused on affordability, convenience, and exceptional customer experience. We serve thousands of customers every day across our branches and online platforms.

Whether you’re starting your first job or building on years of experience, we offer clear growth paths, hands-on training, and a supportive, team-first culture. Our colleagues have grown from store attendants to branch managers and head office leaders.

We also collaborate with leading retailers such as Quickmart, Naivas, Magunas, Powerstar, and Anturskeys on selected projects and talent partnerships, creating even more opportunities for our team members.

We hire based on attitude and potential as much as experience. If you’re hardworking, honest, and willing to learn, there is a place for you at QuickRetail.

How Our Hiring Process Works

1

Submit Your Application

Share your basic details, preferred location, and role of interest using the form below. You can apply even if the exact role you want is not listed yet.

2

Screening Call or SMS

Our recruitment team reviews your application. If there is a match, we will reach out by phone or SMS to confirm your details and availability.

3

Branch or Virtual Interview

You may be invited for an interview at a nearby branch or virtually. We look at your attitude, reliability, and willingness to serve customers.

4

Offer & Onboarding

Successful candidates receive an official offer. We then take you through orientation, training, and assign you to a branch or department.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need previous retail experience?

Experience is an added advantage, but not always required for entry-level roles. We provide training and support to help you succeed.

How will I know if I am successful?

Our recruitment team will contact shortlisted candidates by phone, SMS, or email. Due to the high number of applications, we may not respond to every applicant.

Are there any recruitment fees?

We do not charge cash recruitment fees. If a role requires a small processing fee, it will only be paid through our official digital payment link.

Can I apply for more than one location?

Yes. You can select “Any location” in the form if you are open to different towns or branches.
